Privacy Policy

CamPDFScanner is committed to protecting your privacy.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from users of the app.

All actions, such as image capturing, importing, and PDF generation, are performed locally on your device.

No data is uploaded, analyzed, or stored outside your device.

The app does not require any login, sign-up, or personal information to use any feature.

When you use sharing features, the files are shared using your device's native sharing capabilities, which operate under their own privacy policies.
